Goose Creek Advisors is a mergers and acquisitions advisory business focused on the middle market in Virginia and throughout the Mid-Atlantic states. If you are interested in acquiring a business or if you are considering selling your business we are in a position to aid you in your efforts.

What makes us unique?

Our clients

Our services

Buy side

We will identify qualified acquisitions based on your criteria. We will perform detailed due diligence, provide financial and transactional analysis, advise on and arrange financing as well as designing and helping implement a business integration plan post acquisition.

Sell side

We will perform the financial and structural analysis to appropriately value your business and prepare it for sale. We will then develop a targeted list of qualified buyers and discreetly market the opportunity in a way that minimizes disruption to your business. We will then work with both the buyer and the seller to reach the appropriate transaction structure.

Valuation & financial advisory

If you need to gain an understanding of the value of your business we are here to help. Whether for bank financing, succession planning, or an insurance issue we will provide a confidential and detailed valuation. We are active in the market and know how a business is valued beyond the balance sheet.
If you already have an acquisition target in mind and are seeking ways in which to optimize the financing of the transaction we can help. We work with you and our network of capital providers to provide the optimal financing solution.
